About the COERbuoy platform

This software was developed at the Center for Ocean Renewable Energies (COER) of Maynooth University, Ireland in collaboration with CorPower AB, Sweden. It was funded by the Science Foundation Ireland (SFI) and supported by Marine Renewable Enegeries Ireland (MaREI).
