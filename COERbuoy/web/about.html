About coerPower Demonstrator

This software was developed at the Center for Ocean Renewable Energies (COER) of Maynooth University, Ireland in collaboration with CorPower AB, Sweden. It was funded by the Science Foudnation Ireland (SFI) and supported by Marine Renewable Enegeries Ireland (MaREI).
